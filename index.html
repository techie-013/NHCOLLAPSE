KEY FINDINGS & PATTERNS

Temporal Pattern

2024 saw peak failures (4 major collapses), with 2025 following closely (5 cases). Monsoon months (June-July) critical.

Geographic Hotspots

Bihar (2), Kerala (2), Delhi (2) show highest vulnerability. Coastal and seismic zones overrepresented.

Most Common Causes

Geotechnical (30%), Construction (30%), Maintenance (20%) - all preventable with proper protocols.
